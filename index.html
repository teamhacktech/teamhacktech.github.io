About Hacktech 2024

Hacktech by Caltech is SoCal's premier annual intercollegiate hackathon. This is our 10-th year hosting Hacktech so we won't disappoint! Hacktech will be covering transportation, food, and accommodation for all hackers coming from the SoCal area. For those not in the immediate vicinity, worry not! You can still tune in online to compete.

This year, our theme is social good. Our three verticals are sustainability, public health, and interactive multimedia.

Hackers can compete in teams of 1-4 members, and will be split into 3 divisions: High School, Beginner, and Advanced. Participants will have 36 hours (4/19/24 9:00pm PST - 4/21/24 9:00am PST) to complete their project and create software or hardware that relates to this year's theme: social good. Winners of the High School and Beginner categories, as well as the top two finishers of the Advanced category, will each win prizes!

FAQ

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is where your ideas can come to life in a short period of time. There's food, speakers, tech talkers, recruiters, and of course, tons of building!

Is Hacktech in-person or online?

Hacktech is hybrid this year. We will be selecting some of the top applicants from the local SoCal colleges (UCLA, USC, Claremont colleges, etc.) to attend in-person.

Who can participate?

College/university and high school students.

Is there a registration fee?

No! Hacktech registration is free.

What are your prizes?

We will be giving away $10,000 in prizes!
