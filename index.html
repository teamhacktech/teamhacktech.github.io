FAQ

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is where your ideas can come to life in a short period of time. There's food, speakers, tech talkers, recruiters, and of course, tons of building!

Is Hacktech in-person or online?

Hacktech is hybrid this year. We will be selecting some of the top applicants from the local SoCal colleges (UCLA, USC, Claremont colleges, etc.) to attend in-person.

Who can participate?

College/university students.
